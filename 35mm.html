Louis Falisse is a Communication Designer from Brussels, Belgium. Louis practices graphic, editorial, and web design with a passion in photography, architecture, and product design. Inspired by European art movements such as brutalism and bauhaus, Louis’ work is intentional and functional with an emphasis on proportion and alignment.

35 MM FILM AND MINOLTA'S SRT-101 IN THE LES

Editorial (Wiki)

December 2018

This book's is about 'my' photography in the Lower East Side, more precisely, my camera and the locations I enjoy photographing; Alphabet City and Chinatown. The content was sourced from Wikipedia and my own photography. The book incorporates a visual design system seen in the table of contents and title pages. This system is echoed on the front & back covers.

Guidelines:
— Your book format must be one of the following: 4.25 x 6.88 in (Pocket size) 5.5 x 8.5 in (Digest) 7.44 x 9.68 in (Crown Quatro)
— You must use fonts from the suggested list only
— You may use max. two different typeface
— Your text must be minimum of 15,000 words
— You must use minimum of 20 images, only in Black & White. (No color image)
— Your images should be minimum 150ppi at 100% scale
— Black & White only for the interior of the book
— Your cover must contain the Title, and name of the Author, if applicable
— You may use color for the cover
