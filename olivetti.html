Louis Falisse is a Communication Designer from Brussels, Belgium. Louis practices graphic, editorial, and web design with a passion in photography, architecture, and product design. Inspired by European art movements such as brutalism and bauhaus, Louis’ work is intentional and functional with an emphasis on proportion and alignment.

Winnow: Olivetti

Editorial Publication

This first issue of ‘winnow’ focuses on ‘Olivetti’, a renowned designer and manufacturer of typewriters and accounting machines. Olivetti was extremely successful throughout the 20th century and pioneered a variety of ideas, designs, and technologies. This collection of images and texts intends to give the audience a better understanding of this heritage brand.
